4. SMS Auto-Import

4.1 Opt-in Only

The SMS Auto-Import feature is entirely optional. It is activated only when you explicitly grant the READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions when prompted on the Dashboard. You may dismiss the prompt or revoke the permission at any time without affecting any other app functionality.

4.2 What It Does

When you enable SMS Auto-Import, DhanTrack will:

  • Perform a one-time historical scan of your SMS inbox for the last 90 days, reading only messages from recognised Indian bank senders
  • Automatically create expense transactions from parsed bank debit SMS
  • Continue listening for and processing incoming bank SMS in real time
  • Refresh the last 3 days of bank SMS when you pull-to-refresh on the Dashboard

4.3 On-Device Processing

All SMS content is processed entirely on your device. Raw SMS message bodies are never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Only the extracted transaction data (amount, merchant, category, date) is stored locally and synced to Firebase — identical to a manually-entered transaction.

4.4 Accuracy

SMS parsing is performed using pattern matching and may not be 100% accurate. Merchant names, categories, and amounts extracted from SMS are best-effort approximations. You are responsible for reviewing and correcting any auto-imported transactions. DhanTrack is not liable for inaccuracies in SMS-imported data.

4.5 Revoking Access

You can revoke SMS permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → DhanTrack → Permissions → SMS. DhanTrack will immediately stop reading or receiving SMS. Previously imported transactions will remain in the app.
