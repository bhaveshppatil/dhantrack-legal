Privacy Policy

DhanTrack – Budget & Money Manager  ·  Effective: March 8, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 5, 2026

DhanTrack is committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Display Name — entered by you or pulled from your Google account
  • Email Address — used for authentication and account identification
  • Phone Number — only when you use Phone/OTP sign-in; used solely for verification and not stored beyond Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase UID — a unique identifier to associate your data in the cloud

Financial Data (entered by you or imported from SMS)

  • Transactions — description, category, amount, and date
  • Budgets — category name, spending limit, and amount spent
  • Account Balances — bank name, account type (Savings/Demat), and monthly balance snapshot

We do not collect bank account numbers, card numbers, PINs, tax IDs, income details, or any sensitive financial credentials.

SMS Data (optional — only with your permission)

SMS access is entirely optional. This feature is only activated if you explicitly grant the SMS permission when prompted. You can dismiss the prompt and use DhanTrack without ever enabling it.

If you grant the READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS permissions, DhanTrack will:

  • Scan your SMS inbox for messages received in the last 90 days from recognised Indian bank senders (e.g., HDFCBK, SBIINB, ICICIB)
  • Parse those messages to extract the transaction amount, merchant name, and date — and automatically create expense entries
  • Listen for incoming bank SMS in real time and auto-log new debit transactions as they arrive
  • Perform a lightweight refresh of the last 3 days of bank SMS whenever you pull-to-refresh on the dashboard

What we do NOT do with SMS:

  • We do not read, store, or transmit personal SMS, OTPs, non-bank messages, or any message content beyond what is needed to extract a transaction amount and merchant
  • Raw SMS message bodies are processed entirely on-device and are never sent to our servers or any third party
  • Only the parsed transaction fields (amount, merchant, category, date) are saved locally and synced to Firebase — identical to a manually-entered transaction
  • Non-bank SMS are filtered out immediately and never accessed

A deduplication key is stored in a local SharedPreferences file to prevent the same SMS from creating duplicate transactions.

Preferences

  • Currency — your selected display currency (default: INR)
  • SMS import preference — whether you have granted or dismissed the SMS permission prompt

Usage & Diagnostic Data

  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous event data including screen views, feature usage, button interactions, and aggregated transaction amounts. Events are associated with your Firebase UID, not your name or email.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports including device model, OS version, app version, and your email address (set as a diagnostic key) to help us identify and fix bugs.

Device & Ad Identifiers

Google Play Services libraries bundled with the app may access the Android Advertising ID. DhanTrack does not use these identifiers for advertising. They are incidentally requested by the underlying Google Play Services SDK and are not used or shared by us.

2. App Permissions

The following permissions may be requested by DhanTrack:

  • READ_SMS — Optional. Reads bank transaction SMS from your inbox to auto-import expense entries. Only SMS from recognised Indian bank senders are processed.
  • RECEIVE_SMS — Optional. Receives incoming bank SMS in real time to automatically log new debit transactions as they arrive.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Optional (Android 13+). Allows DhanTrack to send budget alerts, transaction confirmations, spending summaries, and reminders.
  • INTERNET — Required for Firebase cloud sync, authentication, and analytics.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Allows the background sync worker to reschedule after a device restart.

You can revoke any optional permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → DhanTrack → Permissions.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store, display, and back up your financial data
  • To auto-import bank transactions from SMS (only with your permission)
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase Realtime Database
  • To restore your data when you log in on a new device
  • To run a 24-hour background sync to keep your cloud backup current
  • To send budget alerts, notifications, spending summaries, and reminders (only with your permission)
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To understand feature usage and improve the app experience

4. Data Storage & Security

On Your Device

Your data is stored in a local SQLite database (Room) and encrypted DataStore. Both are excluded from Android device backups for your security.

Home Screen Widget

If you add the DhanTrack widget to your home screen, it reads your locally stored balance and transaction summary. No additional data is collected or transmitted by the widget.

In the Cloud

Your data is synced to Firebase Realtime Database under a path scoped exclusively to your UID (users/{your-uid}/). Only you — when authenticated — can read or write your data.

SMS Processing

All SMS parsing and processing happens entirely on your device. Raw SMS content is never sent to Firebase, our servers, or any third party.

Security Measures

  • All Firebase communication uses TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication via Firebase Authentication (OAuth 2.0)
  • Cloud data access protected by Firebase Security Rules
  • Session data stored in encrypted DataStore
  • SMS data processed entirely on-device — never transmitted

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, DhanTrack may send you the following types of push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging:

  • Budget alerts — when you approach or exceed a spending limit
  • Transaction confirmations — when a transaction is auto-imported from SMS
  • Daily reminders — a gentle nudge to log today's expenses
  • Weekly spending summaries — your week in numbers
  • Month-end summaries — total spend and top category for the month
  • New month reminders — prompt to review budgets at month start
  • Savings streak alerts — celebrate days without overspending
  • Unusual large transaction alerts — a heads-up for unexpectedly high SMS-imported amounts

You can disable all notifications at any time via Android Settings → Apps → DhanTrack → Notifications.

7. Data Retention

  • Active account: Data retained as long as your account exists
  • Account deletion: You can delete your account directly from the Profile screen. Firebase Auth record is removed immediately; Realtime Database data is deleted immediately upon confirmation; local data is removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Analytics: Retained by Google for up to 14 months
  • Crash reports: Retained by Crashlytics for 90 days
  • SMS deduplication keys: Stored in local SharedPreferences and removed when you uninstall the app or clear app data

8. Your Rights

  • Access — all your data is visible within the app
  • Edit — update name and financial data at any time
  • Delete account — delete your account and all associated data from Profile → Delete Account
  • Delete individual records — swipe to delete any transaction, or remove budgets and balances in-app
  • Portability — contact us to request a data export
  • Revoke SMS permission — go to Android Settings → Apps → DhanTrack → Permissions → SMS
  • Opt out of notifications — disable via Android Settings → Apps → DhanTrack → Notifications
  • Opt out of Analytics — adjust your device's advertising settings or contact us

9. Children's Privacy

DhanTrack is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date above and notify users through the app. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
