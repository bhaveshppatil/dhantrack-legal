🗑️ Delete Account & Data

DhanTrack – Budget & Money Manager
Last Updated: March 29, 2026
You have full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated data directly inside the DhanTrack app, or by submitting a request below.

⚡ Option 1 — Delete Directly in the App Instant

The fastest way to delete your account and all data is from within the app:

1
Open DhanTrack and sign in to your account.
2
Tap the Profile tab (bottom navigation).
3
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account".
4
Confirm the deletion in the dialog. Your account and all data will be permanently erased immediately.

What gets deleted instantly:

  • All transactions, budgets, and balance records
  • Your account profile and session data
  • All cloud-synced data on Firebase Realtime Database
  • Your Firebase Authentication account

📧 Option 2 — Submit a Deletion Request

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion via email, tap the button below. It will open your email client with a pre-filled message — just send it.

⚠️ Please send the request from the email address associated with your DhanTrack account so we can verify ownership.
📨 Send Deletion Request Email

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 7 business days. All data listed above will be permanently removed.

🔍 What Data Is Deleted

  • Transactions — all income and expense records
  • Budgets — monthly budget configurations
  • Account balance — starting balance and adjustments
  • Profile info — display name, email, currency preference
  • Cloud data — entire Firebase Realtime Database entry (users/{uid})
  • Auth account — Firebase Authentication user record

Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics events (Firebase Analytics) may be retained as they cannot be linked back to you after account deletion.
